Direction Analysis

For Ana (Casual)

Directions 1, 3, and 4 offer the fastest path to a private room. Direction 5 adds social energy that may help groups feel connected before the game starts.

For Marko (Competitive)

Directions 2 and 5 surface rank and stats prominently. Direction 5 is the strongest esports read — rank banner is the first thing you see.

Recommendation

Direction 3 (Compact Cards) or Direction 5 (Competitive) for MVP. Both balance the three entry points while serving the dominant use case clearly.
